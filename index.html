This is a clock for Warframe's Plains of Eidolon Landscape expansion.
PoE's in-game day/night cycle has 100 minutes of day, and 50 minutes of night.
Since the whole cycle (150 minutes) is not an easy percentage or multiple of our 60-minute hours, it can be difficult to keep track of the time, and there is no in-game clock.
The site will also attempt to notify you when the Eidolons appear in the Plains.
Each Eidolon hour is 6.25 minutes of real time.

It is synchronised to within a minute of the Warframe servers (as they only provide minute-level resolution).
